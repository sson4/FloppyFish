Floppy Fish

  • High Concept: Fish that jump through pipe. Originally, the pipe were suppose to be frying pans, but I couldn't figure out why the game kept on crashing when using frying pan png.
  • Player Experience Goals: Addiction and frustration.
  • Genre: Moblie app game that you can quickly play on the go.
  • Game play: The game start up immediately when load. The goal of the game is to jump through pipe. Left-Click on mouse to jump. Jump too high or take too low, the game with restart. If your fish hit the pipe, the game will restart. Each pipe you jump through will increment the score by 1. See how high you can get with score. The game can be pause at anytime.
  • Prototype goal: Jumping and side scrolling.
  • Features: Similar features to Flappy bird but without the annoying jumping sound so player can concentrate on the pipe.
  • Setting/Premise: Setting is underwater since you are play as a fish.
  • Story: You are a fish and you must swim and by swim I mean jump through obstacle to go home. Original plan was suppose to be a story about a fish out of water that must flopping around to avoid hot frying pan because human want to the fish, but couldn't get the frying pan to work.
  • Target Audience: phone user.
  • Play time: 2 to 3 mins at most but the game can be highly addicting.
  • Strategies: Stay focus and don't hit the pipe.
  • Prototype Assets: (fish.png): http://www.clipartbest.com/cartoon-pics-of-fish
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: The game was inspire by the highly popular Flappy Bird mobile app game.
